# Personas

LeadFlow AI serves users who sell but do not want to live inside a complex CRM.

## Rahul Sharma: Sales Representative
Rahul manages many B2B leads and needs fast prioritization. He values table views, filters, pipeline movement, reminders, and email drafts because they reduce daily repetition.

Primary needs:
- Add leads quickly.
- Know who to contact next.
- See history before outreach.
- Draft professional email quickly.

## Priya Mehta: Startup Founder
Priya sells while also running the company. She needs a small-team CRM that surfaces context without requiring a dedicated sales operations person.

Primary needs:
- Understand pipeline health.
- Avoid losing promising leads.
- Use AI to create first drafts and suggestions.
- Keep the tool lightweight.

## Design Implications
- Do not hide core workflows behind decorative screens.
- Keep the dashboard operational, not promotional.
- Make lead detail pages context-rich.
- Make AI output editable and disposable.
