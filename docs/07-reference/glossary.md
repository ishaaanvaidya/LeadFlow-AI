# Glossary

## Terms
- CRM: Customer Relationship Management system.
- Lead: Potential customer relationship.
- Pipeline: Sales process represented as ordered stages.
- Activity: Append-only event in relationship history.
- DAL: Data Access Layer, server-only boundary for business data.
- DTO: Data Transfer Object, safe minimal data shape for UI or API.
- AI Draft: Suggested email text generated by an LLM.
- Lead Score: Hot/Warm/Cold prioritization signal.
- ADR: Architecture Decision Record.
